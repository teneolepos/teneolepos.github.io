Reinvorgating a classic brand to support your health

www.gethapi.org

Strategy / Design / Product Management

Harvard Pilgrim Health Care felt like they were losing touch with their customer base and launched a new campaign to raise awareness about the brand.

Specifically the client wanted to engage individuals, employers and brokers through new communication.

Impact

  • Saw 3% uptick in engagement
  • Reinvigorated brand to be more relevant

Design

I was given the content strategy and asked to think about the best way to incorporate the new, fun campaign with the more tactical company website. We also had to meet a short development timeline while facing challenges of legacy platforms and different vendors.

The main KPI was user engagement as determined by the number of email sign ups and whitepaper downloads. Since both these actions were not dependent on the home website, I determined that a microsite would be suitable for the new campaign content and allow flexibility for development on a separate platform

In order to motivate users to perform these two actions, I kept the sitemap small. Key content pieces were placed at the top of pages to encourage people to see value in signing up and seek access to additional gated content.

To meet quick turnaround times, I designed in an atomic way that could be quickly built in a CMS.

Product Management

I also took on the responsibilities of product manager and content editor in the CMS. I defined the business rules for how form submission and article access would function for the developers. I collaborated with creative to discuss which atomic blocks were appropriate for content and built out the pages in the CMS. Finally, I managed conversations between the different stakeholders and our developer and QA teams to implement changes and successfully launch the site.
