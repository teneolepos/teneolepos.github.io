Research

I have a deep interest in the obesity epidemic in the United States and the systemic levers in place that contribute to the growing problem. My graduate research focuses on areas to address these challenges.

Carbon Foodprint

Developing a standard for the lifecycle analysis of food items in conjunction with understanding how food purchasing behavior changes given knowledge of climate change impact.

SNAP Online Purchasing Pilot

Researching potential of a single marketplace for federally funded food assistance programs with a focus on pre-prepared, nutritionally balanced meals to address food insecurity and promote better nutritional intake in low income populations. Assessing both opportunities to facilitate healthy choices and economic feasibility of transitioning to this model.
