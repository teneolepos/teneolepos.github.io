Social Impact

I have a deep interest in the obesity epidemic in the United States and the systemic levers in place that contribute to the growing problem. My research focuses on areas to address these challenges.

Carbon Foodprint

Developing a standard for the lifecycle analysis of food items. In addition, understanding how food purchasing behavior changes given knowledge of climate change impact.

Depression, Financial Stress and Race

Driven by the current COVID-19 pandemic and the huge financial impact, utilizing the Americans' Changing Lives Survey to understand the association between financial stress and depression. Futhermore assessing whether depression disproportionately affects minority populations.

SNAP Online Purchasing Pilot

Researching potential of a single marketplace for federally funded food assistance programs with a focus on pre-prepared, nutritionally balanced meals to address food insecurity and promote better nutritional intake in low income populations. Assessing both opportunities to facilitate healthy choices and economic feasibility of transitioning to this model.
