Educating patients about Ankylosing Spondylitis

www.monsterpainintheAS.com

Content Strategy / UX / Product Management

Ankylosing Spondylitis (AS) is a hard to diagnose condition and many people confuse it with inflammatory back pain (IBP).

Novartis Pharmaceuticals wanted to launch an unbranded campaign to help educate patients around this condition. Specifically, they hoped to use a quiz to provide guidance and motivate them to speak with their doctor.

Impact

  • 127,000+ quiz completions since launch
  • Work mentioned in Parade, People, MSNBC, Fox News, etc.

Product Strategy

The brand team lacked strategic direction when it came to managing their digital assets. Product requests and updates were based on business or personal perception - not by user needs.

I saw this as a big gap. To help them develop a sustainable model for digital product managment, I took them through a patient-centric design process

Design

Because the campaign is unbranded, I had a lot more freedom in design. The assets centered around a single campaign video. I worked with the UI designer and developers to create a more interactive experience using cutdowns from the video to integrate animation and movement in the site without distracting users from the core educational content.

Quiz and Doctor Finder

The quiz was originally developed by a third party vendor. There was a set criteria on what qualified someone to have AS as well as multiple exit points for those who may only have IBP. To pull the quiz onto our site, I created logic trees and user flows to guide developers.

We also wanted to be helpful to patients by directing them to a rheumatologist using a doctor finder tool from a third party vendor. I researched what type of information patients cared most about when considering a doctor and used the findings to identify the most important fields to drive physcian choice.

We also wanted the look and feel of this tool to be integrated with our campaign. I defined the requirements for a data dump solution in the backend from the third party vendor to allow us the flexbility to design on the frontend.

Product Management

I worked closely with the development team to define the business rules for the quiz and the doctor finder tool.

I then collaborated with the UI designer and the development team to integrate and tweak the animations to deliver an engaging experience to better understand AS.
